About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at UAB, working with Professor Ragib Hasan at UAB Secure & Trustworthy Computing Lab (SECRETLab). My interests lie broadly at the intersection of mobile and wearable computing, human computer interaction, Internet of Things, machine learning, and smart city, with a focus on distracted pedestrian safety, bystanders privacy and emergency management.

Before started my PhD, I worked at Divine IT Limited where I was working in the voice applications in the research and development team. Before that, I worked at JICA in Dhaka Mass Rapid Transit Development Project (Automated fare system for Bangladesh Road Transport Corporation and Bangladesh Railway).

I previously received a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Dhaka (DU), Bangladesh. In my undergraduate thesis, I was fortunate to work with Professor Hafiz Md. Hasan Babu on reversible fault tolerant programmable array logic.
